FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

What is TAG Personalized guidance?
The decision to buy or lease a car is a complex financial choice that can significantly impact an individual's financial well-being. Many people struggle with understanding the nuances of these options and making the right decision based on their unique circumstances. TAG (Personalized Car Finance Advisor) aims to simplify this decision-making process and provide personalized guidance to users.
How do I fill the form?
    As of now, the database collection contains limited data. The form is designed to retrieve information based on the available data, So to test its functionality, please use the following details:
  • 1. **Pickup and Dropoff Dates:** Choose any preferred dates, ensuring the dropoff date is after the pickup date.
  • 2. **Expected Mileage:** User expected mileage
  • 3. **Type of Car:** Select "Sedan/Suv/Convertible"
  • 4. **Select Car Size:** Choose any option.
  • 5. **Transmission:** Choose "Automatic" or "Manual"
  • 6. **Features:** Tick any features you prefer.
  • 7. **Preferred Company:** Indicate your preferred company or no preference
  • 8. **Expected Price Range:** (Not set up yet)
  • 9. **Services:** Tick any services you prefer.
  • 10. **Credit Score:** Select any credit score option of your choice.

What are the requirements to lease/loan a car?
The requirements to lease/loan a car are as follows:
  • 1. Must be 18 years or older
  • 2. Must have a valid driver's license
  • 3. Must have a valid credit card
  • 4. Must have a valid social security number
  • 5. Must have a valid bank account
